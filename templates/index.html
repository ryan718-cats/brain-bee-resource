About the Brain Bee Assistant

This assistant generates Brain Bee style, scenario-based multiple choice questions using curated science passages. It was built to help learners practice reasoning and comprehension in a friendly, kid-appropriate style.

Categories correspond to topic files used by the Academic Team. The assistant is guided by those passages when creating questions — questions are based only on the selected passage. The difficulty you choose will guide the language and reasoning depth of the generated questions.

Training note:

The question-generation prompts and examples were created from materials designed to mirror the types of content and question formats used by Brain Bee teams. This helps produce practice questions that match the style used by the Academic Team for competitions like the International Brain Bee.
